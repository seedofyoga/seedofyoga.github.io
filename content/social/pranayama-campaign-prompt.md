## Instagram Carousel

### Slides

**Slide 1 — Cover**
"Do you know the different Pranayama techniques ?"

**Slide 2 — Energizing**
Morning — need energy?
→ Kapalabhati
Rapid contractions of the lower belly. Active exhale, passive inhale. 30 pumps per round.
Clears the sinuses. Sharpens focus. Think of it as yoga's version of coffee — without the jitter.

**Slide 3 — Balancing**
Before meditation — need to settle?
→ Nadi Shodhana
Alternate nostril breathing. Inhale right, exhale left. Inhale left, exhale right.
The most accessible pranayama. A good place to start.

**Slide 4 — Calming**
Evening — mind still running?
→ Bhramari
A gentle, continuous hum on the exhale. Feel the vibration in the skull and chest.
Activates the vagus nerve. Measurable effect on anxiety and insomnia.

**Slide 5 — The principle**
The key is matching the technique to the moment.
Morning: activate.
Any time: balance.
Evening: settle.

**Slide 6 — CTA**
